EUROPE is on the brink of despair, devasation, and HORROR. Cities under siege, streets running crimson with BLOOD, and the countryside withered under the weight of FAMINE and disease. Survival is a daily struggle and every breath you draw is a testament to the indomitable WILL to endure amidst unspeakable CRUELTY and unrelenting BRUTALITY, A desperate struggle FOR existence. This ERA will test YOUR resilience, morality, AND very humanity, as you confront the grim realities of WAR, famine, and plague on an inevitable ROAD to your GRAVE.

Bloody Europe is a tabletop roleplaying games that uses MÖRK BORG mechanics set in the XVII century during the thirty years war.
